THE ST. REGIS BAL HARBOUR RESORT – HOTEL POLICIES

CHECK-IN / CHECK-OUT
• Check-in time: 11:00 AM
• Check-out time: 4:00 PM
• Minimum age to check in: 21 years old

SMOKE-FREE PROPERTY
• The St. Regis Bal Harbour Resort is a smoke-free property

PET POLICY
• Pets are welcome
• Maximum number of pets per room: 1
• Maximum pet weight: 25 lbs
• Guests must sign a pet liability waiver at check-in
• Non-refundable pet fee per stay: $200
• Non-refundable pet cleaning fee per night: $20
• Service animals are welcome without fees or documentation

PARKING
• Valet parking only
• Daily valet parking fee: $65
• Hourly valet parking: $35 + tax per hour
• Electric vehicle charging stations available (subject to availability)
• Paid parking guests may use EV charging stations at no additional cost

INCIDENTALS & PAYMENTS
• Property accepts cash, credit cards, and contactless mobile payments
• Incidental authorization required at check-in

ACCESSIBILITY
• Property has elevators
• Accessible rooms and public spaces available
• Accessible features include roll-in showers, grab bars, lowered outlets, visual alarms, and hearing-accessible rooms
• Valet parking available for vehicles equipped for wheelchair access
• Service animals permitted per ADA guidelines
