LOCAL GUIDE – BAL HARBOUR & SURROUNDING AREA

BAL HARBOUR SHOPS
• Luxury open-air shopping destination directly across the street
• Features high-end brands such as Chanel, Gucci, Prada, and Louis Vuitton
• Home to several fine dining restaurants

AIRPORTS
• Miami International Airport (MIA): approximately 16 miles
• Fort Lauderdale–Hollywood International Airport (FLL): approximately 40 minutes by car
